Computer Science - General
TU856

Computer scientists are critical to the global infrastructure that underlie our communications, finance, health, energy, transport and many other vital systems. Are you interested in understanding the algorithmic complexity of problems, automating human intelligence, providing ubiquitous access to information and designing secure and eﬀective software and computer systems? If so, this course will prepare you for work in an exciting industry.

    Award

Computer Science - Infrastructure
TU857

Computer Science (Infrastructure) is about developing software and infrastructure for applications ranging from those on smart devices to those in global corporations. This course provides the skills required to build data centres and networks, and to develop software securely, individually and in teams. Recent graduates work as software developers, software engineers and system administrators.

    Level 8

    Award

Computer Science - International
TU858

This course will provide you with the knowledge and skills to work as a global software engineer or as an application developer, able to develop as needed in an ever-changing global industry. You’ll learn IT skills early on that are required in the international IT industry, including core software design and development skills as well as highly sought-after international skills such as global citizenship, software localisation, entrepreneurship, and creating webinars. You can also select to study a foreign language (German, Chinese or Korean).
    Level
    Level 8

    Award
    Bachelor of Science - Honours

    Duration
